Info

This is the course’s textbook, where each chapter roughly corresponds to a lecture.

I expect students to have read the chapter before the lecture.

It is also a good idea to review the chapter again after the lecture.

Reminder!

Keep in mind that your education is in your own hands: what you get out of the course is commensurate to what you put in. Put in the work!

Here are some suggestions:

  • Read as many times as necessary, don’t just skim the text once.

  • If you don’t understand, use additional resources. Google, Wikipedia, other textbooks, …

  • If you still don’t understand something, ask! In the lecture, in the work groups, your fellow students, … We’re in this together.
